Forum 2024

Forum 2024 information

9–11 September 2024, Baku – Khankendi – Shusha. Official addresses, the programme, presentations and participant impressions are published in this section.

The Forum was organized by the World Association of Azerbaijani Scientists (WAAS), with the support of the State Committee on Diaspora Affairs of the Republic of Azerbaijan (SCDA) and the Ministry of Science and Education of the Republic of Azerbaijan (MSE).
