First Forum of Azerbaijani Scientists Living Abroad — September 2024

Forum 2024 information

The First Forum of Azerbaijani Scientists Living Abroad was held on 9–11 September 2024 in Baku, Khankendi and Shusha. This section publishes official addresses, the programme, presentations and participant impressions, organized by WAAS with support from SCDA and MSE.
