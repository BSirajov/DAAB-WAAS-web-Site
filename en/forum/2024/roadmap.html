📋Roadmap for the development ofAzerbaijani science and education

Strategic goals prepared on the basis of forum participants' proposals

These proposals cover a wide range of measures intended for the development of Azerbaijani science and education. They highlight educational initiatives such as joint and staged degree programmes with foreign and local faculty, certified courses, cooperation with international universities and dual-degree programmes. In the scientific field they include publications in Scopus-indexed journals, placements in international laboratories, research centres in artificial intelligence and digital technologies, patenting and translation projects.

Digital tools are also proposed — virtual universities, online platforms, YouTube channels and knowledge-exchange systems — together with diaspora participation, cooperation through ERASMUS and international grant mechanisms. In the cultural and humanitarian sphere, scientific missions related to Karabakh and the international promotion of Azerbaijani music are noted. The proposals further cover modern approaches in bioinformatics, medical technologies, green energy and regional development, and activities that encourage international integration.

This roadmap sets out strategic goals that strengthen the country's integration into the global scientific arena, mobilise diaspora potential effectively and shape a sustainable, innovation-based model of development.

🎓Education and academic development

  • Organisation of joint and staged degree programmes
  • Certified intensive courses, summer and winter schools
  • Implementation of dual-degree and work-integrated learning models
  • Establishment of new faculties and research centres in the regions
  • Teaching transliteration and translation methodology
  • Student and faculty exchange programmes with foreign universities

🔬Scientific research and publications

  • Support for publishing articles on Scopus and other leading platforms
  • Establishment of internationally ranked scientific journals
  • Thematic research centres in toponymy, migration history, tribal culture, etc.
  • Establishment of research centres linked to Karabakh University
  • Scientific monographs, anthologies, encyclopaedias and translation projects

💻Digital platforms and virtual infrastructure

  • Establishment of virtual universities
  • Online course and certification systems
  • Databases and knowledge-exchange platforms for scientists
  • Dissemination of educational content on YouTube and social media

🌍International cooperation and diaspora integration

  • Recognition of scientists active abroad and engagement in cooperation
  • Participation in ERASMUS, Horizon Europe and other grant programmes
  • Building strategic ties with diaspora organisations abroad
  • Teaching Azerbaijani language and literature in foreign countries
  • Organising mutual visits of scientists and students

👥Youth and public outreach

  • Scientists' talks and motivation events in schools and universities
  • Meetings and training that spark scientific interest among young people
  • Mentoring programmes (with both local and diaspora scientists)

🧬Health and biotechnology

  • Establishment of bioinformatics and genetic research centres
  • Building medical databases
  • Scientific training and fellowship programmes for physicians and researchers

🎭Arts, language and cultural heritage

  • Promotion and dissemination of Azerbaijani music and composers' works
  • Borchali anthology and other literary projects by region
  • Research and publication on Turkic toponyms
  • Introducing Azerbaijani writers' works abroad
  • Encyclopaedias and monographs on migration heritage

⚖️Governance, policy and legal mechanisms

  • Strategic planning and policy papers in education and science
  • Reforms in PhD education and the supervision system
  • Mechanisms for diploma recognition and legislative support

⚡Energy, environment and regional development

  • Projects in green energy, solar technologies and agrotourism
  • Digital solutions for power stations
  • Development models for Karabakh and other regions based on natural resources
