Why Support WAAS

Your partnership creates lasting impact

WAAS turns every contribution into scientific progress and long-term capacity for Azerbaijan.

🎓

Scholarships & Fellowships

Funding early-career Azerbaijani researchers to pursue doctoral and postdoctoral programmes at world-leading institutions.

🔬

Research Collaborations

Bridging diaspora experts with Azerbaijani universities and Azerbaijani National Academy of Sciences to co-author publications, share labs, and transfer know-how.

🌐

International Forums

Hosting the regular Forum of Azerbaijani Scientists Abroad — a flagship gathering that shapes the national R&D agenda.

🏗️

Karabakh Reconstruction

Supporting scientific and technical contributions to the reconstruction of liberated Karabakh territories through expert mobilisation.

📚

Publications & Open Access

Promoting knowledge sharing through scientific journals, expert reports, and open-access materials in Azerbaijani and English.

🤝

Diaspora Network

Maintaining a living registry of Azerbaijani scientists worldwide and facilitating mentorship, mobility, and career pathways.
