World Association of Azerbaijani Scientists (WAAS)

WAAS is a non-governmental organization that operates as a union of Azerbaijani intellectuals living in various countries around the world.

WAAS supports Azerbaijan in developing and implementing strategic plans related to science and technology, energy, education, healthcare, social sciences and culture.
