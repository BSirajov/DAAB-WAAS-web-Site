Teaching Innovation
In 1991-92, I have entirely redesigned the course “Statistics for business and social sciences” at the
Azerbaijan Economic University. As I found nothing appropriate being currently published, I combined
several textbooks into a new custom courseware entitled Statistical methods for business and social
sciences. This courseware has being used since then, and combines methods from calculus, algebra and
statistical inference to resolve practical problems in business and economics.
I have created  new WebCT courses (S1A03, S1CC3, S3Y03) at McMaster University (2005-2006).
For STAT 231 Sections 1&2, I used a video conferencing technology at the University of Waterloo
(Fall 2008 and Winter 2009).
