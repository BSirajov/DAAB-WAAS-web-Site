Forum programme

Full schedule of the opening session, plenary sessions, presentations and closing events in Baku, Khankendi and Shusha, 9–11 September 2024.
📅 Programme
  • 9 September 2024 — Baku
  • 10 September 2024 — Strategic sessions
  • 11 September 2024 — Khankendi and Shusha

9 September 2024 — Baku

TimeEvent
08:00 – 09:30Visit to the tomb of National Leader Heydar Aliyev and the Alley of Martyrs
10:00 – 11:00Registration of forum participants and distribution of lapel badges
11:00 – 12:30OPENING SESSION (Moderator: Academician Rafael HUSEYNOV)
11:00 – 11:04National Anthem of the Republic of Azerbaijan
11:04 – 11:05A one-minute silence in memory of our martyrs who sacrificed their lives for the territorial integrity of the Republic of Azerbaijan

12:45 – 14:00LUNCH BREAK
14:00 – 15:30FIRST PLENARY SESSION, Moderator: Dr. Dinara ABBASOVA (Austria)

Discussion topic: The role of scientists in the internationalisation of science and higher education in Azerbaijan

  • What role do internationalisation strategies play—including faculty and student mobility and aligning curricula with labour-market needs—in producing graduates with modern knowledge and skills?
  • What functions can scientists living abroad perform in developing science and higher education in Azerbaijan and attracting international students and professors?

Participants:

  • Hafiz PASHAYEV, Rector of ADA University
  • Adalat MURADOV, Rector of Azerbaijan State University of Economics
  • Vilayat VALIYEV, Rector of Azerbaijan Technical University
  • Hamlet ISAKHANLI, Founder of Khazar University
  • Rufat AZIZOV, Rector of Sumgayit State University during the forum; from 2025, Rector of Azerbaijan State University of Oil and Industry
  • Ceyran MAHMUDOVA, Rector of Azerbaijan State University of Culture and Arts
TimeEvent
15:30 – 16:00COFFEE BREAK
16:00 – 17:30SECOND PLENARY SESSION, Moderator: Prof. Dr. Mesud AFANDIYEV (Germany)

Discussion topic: Building a global academic network — new approaches to developing local universities and research institutes

  • How can the role of scientists living abroad be strengthened in connecting universities and research institutes operating in Azerbaijan with international academic networks?
  • China's experience is a positive example of benefiting from diaspora scientists. How do our scientists abroad see establishing such a model for Azerbaijan?

Participants:

  • Elmar GASIMOV, Rector of Baku Higher Oil School
  • Gulchohra MAMMADOVA, Rector of Azerbaijan State University of Architecture and Construction
  • Vazeh ASKEROV, Acting Rector of Azerbaijan State University of Oil and Industry during the forum
  • Elchin BABAYEV, Rector of Baku State University
  • Yagub PIRIYEV, Rector of Baku Engineering University
  • Elbrus ISAYEV, Rector of Nakhchivan State University

In these sessions

  • Discussions were held around sample short-, medium- and long-term strategies, and the most suitable options for implementation were selected.
  • Documents were produced reflecting the group's priority strategic directions and explaining why they were appropriate, also taking into account issues raised in the addresses of state officials and university leaders.

The closing session is intended to discuss and adopt short-, medium- and long-term strategies. If time constraints or other reasons prevent strategies from being finalised during the forum, designated WAAS members and local participants will work offline in groups on the outcomes obtained.
