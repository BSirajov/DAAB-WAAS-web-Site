1. Overview

WAAS Sponsorship & Strategic Partnership Proposal

The World Association of Azerbaijani Scientists (WAAS) unites Azerbaijani scientists, researchers, engineers, physicians, legal scholars, educators, and other highly qualified professionals across the world on a single international scientific and intellectual platform.

WAAS’s core mission is to channel the knowledge, experience, and professional potential of Azerbaijani scientists abroad toward the development of science, education, technology, innovation, and human capital in Azerbaijan. We therefore invite your company to become a strategic partner and sponsor of WAAS.

2. Recent impact

Connecting diaspora science with Azerbaijan

Through international gatherings, university partnerships, and ongoing programmes, WAAS has brought together scientists and experts from more than 20 countries. These initiatives have produced numerous proposals aimed at strengthening Azerbaijan’s scientific and educational environment.

  • Science and technology
  • Energy and green transition
  • Health and biotech
  • Higher education partnerships
  • Artificial intelligence
  • International law
  • Social and humanities
  • Karabakh University

3. Strategic priorities

What your partnership helps deliver

Sponsorship supports WAAS programmes that turn diaspora expertise into lasting outcomes for Azerbaijan.

  • Mobilise the potential of Azerbaijani scientists abroad in a systematic way
  • Strengthen international cooperation with Azerbaijani universities and research institutions
  • Create mentoring and training opportunities for young scientists and students
  • Build new cooperation mechanisms between industry, business, and academia
  • Support international links for Karabakh University and regional universities
  • Increase Azerbaijan’s international visibility in science and innovation

4. Benefits for sponsors

What your partnership delivers — for you and for Azerbaijan

Strengthen your brand while gaining access to international expertise, research partnerships, innovation channels etc.

🌐

Brand visibility & the WAAS platform

Your logo across WAAS materials, the website, media, and official publications and events. As a partner of an international scientific association that shapes Azerbaijan’s research and education agenda, your company stands alongside the country’s leading intellectual community on the global stage.

🏅

Reputation & accountability

Recognition as a trusted partner investing in science, education, and innovation — with measurable social impact, formal acknowledgment, listing in the annual report, and direct access to the WAAS Executive Board.

🤝

Diaspora network & strategic connections

Direct links to scientists and experts from 20+ countries, universities, and research centres; access to WAAS’s living scientist registry, mentorship and academic mobility pathways, and high-level dialogue with leading scientists and decision-makers.

🔬

Research collaboration & expertise

Bridges between diaspora scholars and Azerbaijani universities and the National Academy of Sciences — co-authored publications, shared laboratories, knowledge transfer, and advisory or research partnerships aligned with your sector.

📚

Innovation, publications & open science

Joint initiatives in priority fields from AI and energy to health sciences; sponsorship visibility through expert reports, scientific publications, and open-access knowledge sharing in Azerbaijani and English.

🎓

Human capital & the Karabakh agenda

Scholarships and doctoral or postdoctoral pathways for young researchers; mentoring and talent development. Support for mobilising scientific and technical expertise in Karabakh’s reconstruction and for Karabakh University and regional institutions — a lasting investment in Azerbaijan’s knowledge economy.
